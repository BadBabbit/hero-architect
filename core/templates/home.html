Welcome to HeroArchitect

Speak, and your Hero shall rise!

Create a D&D5e Character, using only your words!

Converse with our AI, HeroArchitect, to shape your vision for your character into reality. The AI will guide you through all the necessary steps to create your character.

The AI will ask you a series of questions about your character - some of them relating to the abilities and skills of your character, and other questions relating to your character's personality, history, and appearance. HeroArchitect will walk you through all the steps required to turn your concept into a full-fledged Hero.

After your conversation is concluded, the AI will intelligently fill your character sheet details on your behalf!

No idea how to create a character? No problem!

You may have a character concept, but have no idea how to turn it into reality. That's okay! That's what HeroArchitect is for.

If all you know about your character is that you want to play a level 1 Wizard, that's more than enough to start you off!

Even if you simply have a concept in mind, that's okay too! For example, you may know that you want your character to lurk in the shadows and be a deceptive person of many faces. HeroArchitect will take that concept and turn it into a fully-realised character with you.

Words are your building blocks, and HeroArchitect is your... well, architect!

Give as much or as little detail as you like!
Here are some examples of how to start off the conversation:
  • "I want to create a character who uses dark magic!"
  • "I'd like my character to be nimble and dexterous, dodging attacks and running along walls to get the better of their opponents."
  • "Help me make the strongest, most savage, most terrifying Barbarian that the world has ever seen! But, also, incorporate that he has a soft-spot for strawberry cake..."
